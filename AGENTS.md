- YOU CANNOT MOVE ON UNTIL THE PLAN IS BULLETPROOF.

- Do not overscope. When implementing, always adhere to scope of the implementation plan markdown files.

- Validate extensively. When given a goal, you cannot sleep until that goal is validated and 100% reached. If not, continue iterating.
- YOU CANNOT MOVE ON UNTIL FUNCTIONALITY IS PERFECTLY VALIDATED.
- Validation includes gather concrete evidence. The only valuable form of evidence is a screenshot image.
- Test for every case, not just running a single simple case then moving on.
- If you notice yourself "going in circles," change your approach.
- When you finish a task and want to show it to the user, run the commands to start development servers or open demos automatically.
